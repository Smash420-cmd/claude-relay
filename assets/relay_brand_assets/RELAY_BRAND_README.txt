RELAY BRANDMARK ASSETS
======================

Colour Palette
--------------
Blue (capsule body):   #4A6CF7
Purple (streams):      #7B2FFF  
Terracotta (streams):  #E8622A
Cyan (text):           #00FFD1
Background:            #000000

Files Included
--------------
relay_brandmark_transparent.png   — Master lockup, transparent background (1865x975px)
relay_brandmark_on_black.png      — Lockup on black background (1865x975px)
relay_brandmark_on_white.png      — Lockup on white background (1865x975px)
relay_icon_1024_transparent.png   — Square app icon, transparent (1024x1024px)
relay_icon_1024_black.png         — Square app icon, black background (1024x1024px)

Usage Notes
-----------
- Preferred background: black (#000000)
- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the /relay text on all sides
- Do not recolour, distort, or add effects to the brandmark
- For small sizes use the icon variants, not the full lockup
